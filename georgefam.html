Welcome to the George Fam Centre

Talking Maps is a series of free and open public events that aims to reignite memories of Christmas Island's past and record them for the future. Each Talking Maps event brings Islanders together at a historic site on Christmas Island to tell stories and collaboratively create a series of maps that capture islandersâ€™ lived experience of the site.

The shops and some of our favourite haunts like the cinema, Snake Pit and Pool were virtually destroyed as were the police quarters and the hospital."

-Jane Yorkston
